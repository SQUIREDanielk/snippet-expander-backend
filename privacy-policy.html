Snippet Expander — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

Snippet Expander is a Chrome extension that lets users create text templates and expand them in any browser text field. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We also store the snippet templates you create (name, shortcut, and content) so they can be synced across your devices and shared with your team.

We do not collect browsing history, keystrokes, or any data from the web pages you visit. The extension only activates when you type a snippet shortcut in a text field.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Snippet Expander service:

Your email and password are used for authentication. Your snippets are stored on our server so they sync across devices and can be shared with team members. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any other purpose.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers hosted by Railway (railway.com). Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text. All communication between the extension and our server uses HTTPS encryption.

Team Features

If you join a team, your email address will be visible to other team members. Snippets you add to a team are accessible to all members of that team.

Local Storage

The extension stores your snippets and authentication token in Chrome's local storage so the extension works offline. This data stays on your device.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual snippets at any time through the extension. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

Third-Party Services

We use Railway (railway.com) for server hosting. No other third-party services receive your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
